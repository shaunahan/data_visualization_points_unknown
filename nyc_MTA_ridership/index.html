Geographical Distribution of NYC Subway Usage and Household Income

This map documents the distribution of ridership by subway station between March 6 and March 20th 2020, as measured through turnstile usage. To contextualize the subway usage, an additional layer of Median Household Income has been added. The original turnstile data is provided by the New York City Metro Transit Authority, and geocoded by the Points Unknown class. The household income data is provided by the U.S. Census Bureau.
